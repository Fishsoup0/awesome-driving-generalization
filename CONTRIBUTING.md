# Contributing

Thank you for helping maintain this research hub.

## Paper suggestions

A paper should satisfy at least one of the following:

- explicitly evaluate distribution shift, corruption, long-tail, or open-world behavior;
- introduce a driving dataset/benchmark with a clear generalization axis;
- propose adaptation, uncertainty, calibration, or safety-aware evaluation;
- connect generation/world modeling to robustness, downstream transfer, or closed-loop utility.

Please provide:

1. title, year, venue, and stable paper URL;
2. official code/project URL when available;
3. sensor modalities and task;
4. distribution shift or deployment failure studied;
5. one sentence explaining why the work belongs here.

## Pull requests

- Edit both the human-readable Markdown and the corresponding CSV entry.
- Prefer official proceedings pages over search-result or aggregator links.
- Do not copy abstracts, figures, or tables without permission.
- Label workshop, preprint, and main-conference papers accurately.
- Run `python scripts/validate_catalog.py` before opening a PR.

## Review criteria

Maintainers verify metadata, scope fit, venue status, and whether claims match the cited source. Inclusion is selective and does not imply endorsement.
